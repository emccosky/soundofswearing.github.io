How does vulgar speech differ from regular speech between languages globally?



A group of three undergraduate students from the University of Texas at Dallas, under the guidance of Dr. Ben Wright tried to understand the effects of emotion and context on languages.



Explore the tabs to learn how we analyzed speech, view our comparative graphs of sound distributions, and understand our findings.



Research Team Bio

We are a team of college students seeking to understand the relationship between the meaning and sound of words. The project was first created as a final project in the honors class "Digital Humanities" at The University of Texas at Dallas.
